Delete your account – Impaxt Alert

If you wish to delete your account, follow these steps within the app:

  1. Open the Impaxt Alert app.
  2. Go to your personal profile page.
  3. Tap the "Delete Account" button at the bottom.
  4. Confirm your action.

Note: Once your account is deleted, all associated data including your email, preferences, and saved alerts will be permanently removed from our servers within 24 hours. This action cannot be undone.

For any assistance, please contact us at support@impaxtalert.com.
